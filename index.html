Shopping Guide

Shopping Guide

Shopping from vendors can yield valuable items and bases for your character. Below are key points to understand how shopping works:

  • Item Level (ilvl): Determined by your character level (clvl) + 5. For example, if you are clvl 10, items will be ilvl 15.
  • Normal Difficulty Caps: Each act in Normal has an ilvl cap:
    • Act 1: ilvl 12
    • Act 2: ilvl 20
    • Act 3: ilvl 25
    • Act 4: ilvl 36
    • Act 5: ilvl 45
  • Item Quality: Vendors sell normal and socketed items early on, transitioning to magic items at higher levels.
  • Exceptional and Elite Items: Starting in Nightmare, vendors can sell exceptional items, and in Hell (Act 3+), elite items may appear.
  • Vendor Upgrades: In Nightmare and Hell, some vendors offer upgraded versions of items, replacing normal ones with exceptional or elite versions.

How to Shop

  • Reset vendors by leaving town via the main entrance, waypoint, portal, or sewer in Act 2.
  • Use portals (e.g., Cow Level in Act 1) for quick resets near NPCs.
  • In Acts 1 and 2, restarting the game can adjust town layouts for faster shopping.
