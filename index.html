Crafting

Crafting Explanation

Think of crafting like cooking a special recipe. The quality of your dish depends on two main things: the chef's skill (your character level) and the quality of the ingredients (the item's level). In Diablo 2, this combined quality is called the "item level" or ilvl. The higher the ilvl, the better the potential affixes (like extra sprinkles or toppings) your crafted item can have.

To calculate the ilvl of a crafted item, take half of your character level and half of the item's level, then add them together. For example, if your character is level 80 (half is 40) and the item is level 60 (half is 30), the crafted item's ilvl would be 40 + 30 = 70.

Ubers Guide

Accessing Uber Tristram

  1. Collect Keys in Hell difficulty:
    • Terror Key: The Countess
    • Hate Key: The Summoner
    • Destruction Key: Nihlathak
  2. Use the Horadric Cube to combine 1 of each key to create the portal to Uber Tristram.

Strategies for Defeating Ubers

Uber Tristram is home to Uber Diablo, Uber Mephisto, and Uber Baal. Here are some strategies to defeat them:

  • Life Tap: Use a weapon with Life Tap or have a Necromancer cast it to sustain your health.
  • Crushing Blow: Gear with Crushing Blow is essential for reducing the bosses' health quickly.
  • Resistances: Max out your resistances, especially fire and lightning, to survive their attacks.
  • Mobility: Use teleport or fast run/walk to avoid being surrounded by minions.
  • Builds: Smiter Paladin, Kicksin Assassin, and Zealot Paladin are popular builds for ubers.

Recommended Gear

  • Weapon: Last Wish, Grief, or a wand with Life Tap charges.
  • Shield: Herald of Zakarum, Stormshield, or a high-resistance shield.
  • Armor: Chains of Honor, Enigma, or Fortitude for damage and survivability.
  • Helm: Guillaume's Face for Crushing Blow, Vampire Gaze for damage reduction.
  • Boots: Goblin Toe or Gore Rider for Crushing Blow.
  • Amulet/Rings: Highlord's Wrath, Raven Frost, and Bul-Kathos' Wedding Band for stats and resistances.
