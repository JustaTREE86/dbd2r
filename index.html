Crafting

Crafting Formula (Explained Like You’re 5)

Imagine you’re baking a super cool cake, and how yummy it turns out depends on two things: how good you are at baking (your level) and how fancy your mixing bowl is (the item’s level). We call this special cake number the “item level,” or ilvl for short.

Here’s how we make it simple:

  • Take your level (how many years you’ve practiced baking) and cut it in half.
  • Take the item’s level (how special your bowl is) and cut it in half too.
  • Add those two halves together, and that’s your cake’s ilvl!

For example: If you’re 6 years old (level 6), half is 3. If your bowl is a level 4 bowl, half is 2. So, 3 + 2 = 5. Your cake’s ilvl is 5! The bigger the ilvl, the more awesome stuff (like sprinkles or chocolate chips) your cake can have on it.
