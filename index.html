Runewords

Runewords

Runewords are powerful combinations of runes inserted into socketed items. Below is a list of popular runewords with their required runes, item types, effects, and recommended bases.

Creating Runewords

To create a runeword, insert the runes in the exact order into a non-magical socketed item of the correct type. The item must have the exact number of sockets required by the runeword.

Ubers Guide

Ubers Guide

Uber Tristram is an endgame challenge. Here’s how to tackle it.

Accessing Uber Tristram

  1. Collect Keys in Hell:
    • Terror Key: The Countess
    • Hate Key: The Summoner
    • Destruction Key: Nihlathak
  2. Combine 1 of each key in the Cube to open the portal.

Strategies

  • Life Tap: Use a wand or curse for sustain.
  • Crushing Blow: Essential for boss damage.
  • Resistances: Max fire and lightning resists.
