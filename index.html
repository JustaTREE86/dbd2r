Crafting

Crafting Formula

Crafted Item Level (ilvl): Crafted ilvl = int(clvl/2) + int(ilvl/2)

Where:

  • clvl = Character level when crafting
  • ilvl = Item level of the base item
  • int() = Rounds down to the nearest integer

Crafted items get 1-4 random affixes based on ilvl, plus the fixed affixes listed below.

Ubers Guide

Accessing Uber Tristram

  1. Collect Keys in Hell difficulty:
    • Terror Key: The Countess
    • Hate Key: The Summoner
    • Destruction Key: Nihlathak
  2. Open portals by combining 1 of each key in the Cube (random portal to Uber Duriel, Uber Izual, or Lilith).
  3. Defeat each boss for:
    • Baal’s Eye (Uber Izual)
    • Mephisto’s Brain (Lilith)
    • Diablo’s Horn (Uber Duriel)
  4. Combine all three organs in the Cube in Harrogath to open Uber Tristram.

Defeating Ubers

Gear for high resistances (75%+), life steal, and Crushing Blow. Target Uber Mephisto first (due to Conviction aura), then Baal, then Diablo. Reward: Hellfire Torch.

Upping Items

Upgrading unique and set items to exceptional or elite versions.

Recipes

  • Unique Items:
    • Normal → Exceptional: 1 Ort + 1 Amn + 1 Perfect Sapphire + Normal Unique Weapon
    • Exceptional → Elite: 1 Lum + 1 Pul + 1 Perfect Emerald + Exceptional Unique Weapon
  • Set Items:
    • Normal → Exceptional: 1 Ko + 1 Lem + 1 Perfect Diamond + Normal Set Armor
    • Exceptional → Elite: 1 Fal + 1 Um + 1 Perfect Sapphire + Exceptional Set Armor

Note: Upping increases level requirements.

Larzuk Socket Quest

Larzuk adds sockets as a reward for completing "Siege on Harrogath" (kill Shenk the Overseer in Act 5).

  • White Items: Adds max sockets based on item type and ilvl.
  • Unique/Set Items: Adds 1 socket.

Tip: Save for ethereal bases or unique weapons like Windforce.
