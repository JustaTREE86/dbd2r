Countess Rune Drops

The Countess can drop the following runes based on difficulty (always kill on /players1 in single player):

  • Normal: El, Eld, Tir, Nef, Eth, Ith, Tal, Ral
  • Nightmare: Ort, Thul, Amn, Sol, Shael, Dol, Hel, Io (Very rare: Lum, Ko)
  • Hell: Lum, Ko, Fal, Lem, Pul, Um, Mal, Ist (Very rare: Gul, Vex, Ohm, Lo)

Hellforge Rune Drops (Act 4 Quest)

  • Normal: El to Amn
  • Nightmare: Sol to Um
  • Hell: Hel to Gul

High Rune Farming

High runes (Sur, Ber, Jah, Cham, Zod) are best found by farming Lower Kurast or Arcane Sanctuary by opening chests, and areas like The Ancient Tunnels or Secret Cow Level in Hell difficulty. Drop chances are extremely rare, so killing many monsters quickly increases your odds.

Runewords

Runewords

Important Tips:

  • WARNING #1: Runewords can only be created in white (normal) or superior socketed items. Magic, rare, set, or unique items will not work.
  • WARNING #2: You cannot add sockets to already socketed items. Using the Horadric Cube gives a 50% chance for the maximum number of sockets.
  • WARNING #3: Always double-check rune order and item type. Many runewords specify particular item classes (e.g., polearms vs. spears).
  • Choose bases with low strength and dexterity requirements for easier equipping.
  • Using the Larzuk quest reward will always grant the maximum number of sockets for that item's ilvl.
  • You can remove items from sockets using a Hel rune and a TP scroll in the Horadric Cube, but the runes or gems will be destroyed.
  • For runeword effects, refer to: Arreat Summit or D2io Runewords.

Bases

Runeword Bases

Notes on Bases:

  • Elite bases can only be found in Hell, with the best location being The Secret Cow Level. Exceptions like Berserker Axe can be found in The Pit, Worldstone Keep, Mausoleum, or Maggot Lair.
  • Many runewords can technically use various bases, but are often only optimal in one or two specific bases.
  • Superior bases, especially with max rolls, are highly valuable.
