Countess Rune Drops

The Countess in the Forgotten Tower (Black Marsh, Act 1) is a reliable source for runes. Always kill on /players1 in Single Player for maximum efficiency. Her rune drop limits are:

  • Normal: El, Eld, Tir, Nef, Eth, Ith, Tal, Ral
  • Nightmare: Ort, Thul, Amn, Sol, Shael, Dol, Hel, Io (Very rare: Lum, Ko)
  • Hell: Lum, Ko, Fal, Lem, Pul, Um, Mal, Ist (Very rare: Gul, Vex, Ohm, Lo)

Hellforge Rune Drops

The Hellforge quest in Act 4 can drop runes up to the following levels:

  • Normal: El to Amn
  • Nightmare: Sol to Um
  • Hell: Hel to Gul

Farming High Runes

The high runes (Sur, Ber, Jah, Cham, Zod) are best found farming areas like Lower Kurast, Arcane Sanctuary, Ancient Tunnels, or the Secret Cow Level in Hell difficulty by opening chests and killing mobs. Drop chances are extremely rare, so prioritize areas with high mob density for better odds.

Runewords

Runewords

Runewords are powerful combinations of runes inserted into socketed items. Below is a list of popular runewords with their required runes, item types, and level requirements.

Creating Runewords

To create a runeword, insert the runes in the exact order into a non-magical (white or superior) socketed item of the correct type with the exact number of sockets required. Warnings:

  • Warning #1: Runewords cannot be made in magic, rare, set, or unique items with sockets.
  • Warning #2: You cannot socket already socketed items. Using the Horadric Cube gives a 50% chance at max sockets.
  • Warning #3: Double-check recipes to ensure correct rune order and item type (e.g., polearm runewords won’t work in spears).

Important Tips

  • Choose bases with low strength and dexterity requirements.
  • Larzuk quest always gives the maximum number of sockets for an item’s ilvl.
  • Buy bases with open sockets from vendors and reset them by leaving town via the main entrance, portal, or waypoint.
  • Use Hel Rune + TP scroll in the Horadric Cube to remove all items from sockets (runes/gems are destroyed).

Shopping Guide

Shopping Guide

Shopping from vendors can yield valuable items and bases for your character. Below are detailed mechanics, tips, and specific items to look for.

Basic Facts about Shopping

  • Terms: ilvl (item level), clvl (character level), alvl (affix level), qlvl (quality level).
  • You cannot shop Ethereal items directly.
  • The ilvl of items is clvl + 5 (e.g., clvl 10 = ilvl 15).
  • Normal Difficulty Caps:
    • Act 1: ilvl 12
    • Act 2: ilvl 20
    • Act 3: ilvl 25
    • Act 4: ilvl 36
    • Act 5: ilvl 45
  • Vendors sell normal/socketed items up to ilvl 25 (clvl 20+). Beyond ilvl 25, only magic items are sold.
  • If another player is in town, the shopping window won’t update when resetting.
  • Item affixes depend on qlvl and ilvl. Use this calculator for details.
  • From Nightmare, exceptional items can appear; from Act 3 Hell, elite items can spawn. Use this shopping calculator for specifics.
  • Vendors have “Nightmare/Hell upgrade” tags, replacing some normal items with exceptional/elite versions in higher difficulties.
  • Items only appear if their qlvl ≤ ilvl (e.g., Ring Mail, qlvl 11, requires clvl 6+ from Charsi in Normal).

How to Shop

  • Reset vendors by leaving town via main entrance, waypoint, portal, or sewer in Act 2.
  • In Act 1 and 5, use Cow Level or mini-uber portals near the NPC for quick resets.
  • In Acts 1 and 2, restart the game to adjust town entrance layout for faster resets (Cow portal is fastest in Act 1).

Ubers Guide

Ubers Guide

Uber Tristram is an endgame challenge. Here’s how to access and defeat it.

Accessing Uber Tristram

  1. Collect Keys in Hell difficulty:
    • Terror Key: The Countess
    • Hate Key: The Summoner
    • Destruction Key: Nihlathak
  2. Combine 1 of each key in the Horadric Cube to open the portal to Uber Tristram.

Strategies for Defeating Ubers

  • Life Tap: Use a weapon with Life Tap or have a Necromancer cast it to sustain your health.
  • Crushing Blow: Gear with Crushing Blow is essential for reducing the bosses' health quickly.
  • Resistances: Max out your resistances, especially fire and lightning, to survive their attacks.
  • Mobility: Use teleport or fast run/walk to avoid being surrounded by minions.
  • Builds: Smiter Paladin, Kicksin Assassin, and Zealot Paladin are popular builds for ubers.
